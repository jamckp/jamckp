Frontend Development

I pay close attention to front-end development best practices and design trends, everything I build is designed to perform flawlessly across a range platforms and devices. Web Design

I build strategic, engaging websites that reinforce your message to your target audience. I understand how visitors engage with your site and can design an intuitive solution that ultimately converts users into customers.

Performance Optimisation

A 1 second delay in loading your page can increase your bounce rate by around 20%. I can help you get the most out of your website by refactoring code for efficiency, caching content and minimising unnecessary work.

Responsive Design

Your website is often a users first interaction with your business, product or service. If they arrive at a site that isn't responsive from a mobile device they may find your site confusing or completely unusable. I use modern web standards and practices to build responsive websites which modify their layout, images and fonts for any device, large or small to ensure fluid reading and navigation without resizing, panning and scrolling.
